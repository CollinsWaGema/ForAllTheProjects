Firecracker Chicken Bites

These little flavor bombs pack heat, sweet, and everything in between. Bite-sized, crispy chicken coated in a sticky chili glaze that snaps, crackles, and pops on your taste buds. Perfect for parties, game nights, or when your dinner needs a little drama.

🔪Ingredients👨🏾‍🍳

  • 500g boneless chicken breast (cut into bite-sized cubes)
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup oil (for frying)

😋Ingredients for the sauce🍅

  • 1/3 cup hot sauce (like Frank’s RedHot or Sriracha)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

🍖Instructions🍗

  1. Coat the Chicken:

    2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge each piece in cornstarch, then dip into the beaten eggs.

  2. Fry Until Golden:

    3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry chicken bites in batches until golden and cooked through. Remove and drain on paper towels.

  3. Make the Sauce:

    4. In a saucepan, combine hot sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes. Bring to a simmer and stir until smooth and slightly thickened (about 5 minutes).

  4. Toss to Coat:

    5. Add the crispy chicken bites into the sauce. Toss until every piece is coated and glistening.

  5. Serve Hot:

    6. Plate up and serve with rice, noodles, or just eat 'em straight with your hands like a savage. Garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if you’re feelin’ fancy.
